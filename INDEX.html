Travel to Cancun

An unforgettable experience.

It is located in the northeast of the Yucatan Peninsula, it is recognized throughout the world for its spectacular white sand beaches and its fascinating sea in turquoise tones, in addition to the excellent air connectivity and its first-class infrastructure, which is why it is considered the leading tourist destination in Latin America.


With its fine white sand beaches, turquoise blue waters, and spectacular coral reefs lying off its shores, it's no surprise that Cancun is one of Mexico's most popular tourist destinations.

Beaches

Cancun and its 10 Blue Flag-certified beaches — most of them located in the hotel zone — remain the most popular vacation spots in Mexico. Here, visitors can enjoy endless days at the beach, while having a refreshing drink. Some of the most beautiful beaches in the hotel zone are Playa Gaviota Azul and Playa Delfines, the latter is a great option for people looking for moderate or intense waves, in addition to the fact that there is the emblematic photographic parador of Cancun.

Things to do in Cancun

Cancun has a wide range of activities that make it a paradise for nature and adventure lovers.

  • Dive or snorkel in the mystical cenotes that are located in its surroundings.
  • Enjoy the sea by kayaking or riding a jet ski.
  • Relax in a spa.
  • Spend the day on a golf course.

Those who are in search of culture can visit the Mayan Museum of Cancun, which houses one of the most significant archaeological collections of the Mayan culture in the country. Be sure to visit the archaeological sites that are located around the city, such as:

  • El Meco
  • El Rey
  • San Miguelito
  • Tulum
  • Cobá
  • Muyil

among others.

Cancun's shopping experience is very varied, as it has luxurious shopping malls and designer stores, as well as picturesque markets. In the hotel zone you will find some of the busiest shopping centers in the city, such as:

  • Marina Puerto Cancún
  • Plaza Kukulcán
  • La Isla Shopping Village
  • Coral Negro
  • Mercado 28

Restaurants and nightlife

Cancun is recognized for its gastronomic offer and is home to some of the best restaurants in the Mexican Caribbean. Here you will find a fantastic mix of flavors from Mexican cuisine to the most refined international cuisine, which are a delight for the senses. The restaurants in the hotel zone offer beautiful views of the Caribbean Sea and the Nichupte Lagoon. After some margaritas you will surely want to continue the party; Cancun's varied entertainment options have given fame to its incomparable nightlife: Bars and clubs with several world-class DJs, live music, shows and lots of fun.

General tips for your visit to Cancun.

  1. Book in advance: Cancun is a popular destination, so book your hotel and flights in advance to get better prices.
  2. Research: Research the different neighborhoods and areas of Cancun to choose the one that best suits your needs.
  3. Clima: Cancún tiene un clima tropical, así que no olvides protegerte del sol y la lluvia.
